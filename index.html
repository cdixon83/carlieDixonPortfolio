    About Me

    I'm a web developer & figure skating coach

    I am a web developer and figure skating coach, with enthusiasm for building unique, responsive and accessible websites. I love to motivate people and am inspired by the work and stories of others. I use this two way passion to cultivate a rich culture for the teams I’m part of and to bring pride to our work.

    When I’m not coding, you can find me at the ice rink, teaching and inspiring kids. My favourite part of teaching figure skating is watching kids grow and develop through their skating journey and to know that I have had a hand in their success.

    I’m excited to continue refining my coding skills with the right team. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in working together.
